Healthy teams optimize for clarity: what to build, how to review it, and how to roll it out safely. This article captures a few practices that have worked well across product orgs.

## Design systems that earn trust

A design system should reduce debate, not add ceremony. Start with tokens, document usage, and ship examples in Storybook (or equivalent) so reviewers can compare against a single source of truth.

## CI as a product

Treat your pipeline like a product: fast feedback, actionable logs, and flaky tests removed quickly. Preview deployments per pull request are one of the highest leverage investments you can make.

## Closing thoughts

If you are early in your journey, pick one bottleneck—build time, test flakiness, or unclear ownership—and solve it completely before layering new process on top.
